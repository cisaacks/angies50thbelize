Belizean Shores Resort

Getting There

Fly to Belize City (BZE). Multiple airlines serve BZE; however, American Airlines often offers the quickest route to Belize.

Hop Flight to San Pedro (Tropic Air)

Book at BZE after customs or online. 15‑minute flight to Ambergris Caye.

Golf Cart Rentals
